About RescueLink

Natural disasters such as floods, earthquakes, and cyclones occur unexpectedly and often cause widespread damage and confusion. During such emergencies, one of the biggest challenges people face is the lack of accurate and timely information.

For example, during the 2018 Kerala floods, many people struggled to access real-time information about safe locations and emergency support.

To address this issue, we propose RescueLink, a centralized disaster response website that provides:

  • Live Safe Zone Maps
  • Relief Camp Locations
  • Emergency Contact Numbers
  • Missing Person Reporting System

Our goal is to reduce panic, improve coordination, and help people access life-saving information quickly during disasters.
